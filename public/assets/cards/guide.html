Welcome to Study Board.

This is a quick tutorial on how to manage and control cards, so you can get the most out of the website.

This is a card. A card can hold anything, whether it's a Spotify playlist, Youtube video or any other type of web content. You can add cards with the button in the bottom right.

In the background there is a video. You can customise this with a YouTube, Vimeo, DailyMotion, or other web video service links. The purpose of the video is not to grab your attention but to provide a nice background to focus.

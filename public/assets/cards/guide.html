Welcome to Study Board.

This is a quick tutorial on how to manage and control cards, so you can get the most out of the website.

This is a card. A card can hold anything, whether it's a Spotify playlist, Youtube video or any other type of web content. You can add cards with the button in the bottom right. You can adjust the size of the cards, but if you want to shrink the whole page (e.g for a large monitor), then use your browser's zoom controls (control/command +/-).

You can also share cards with the button in the bottom-right. Here is an example of a shared StudyBoard. StudyBoards that are shared keep their cards (+ their positions) and the background video, but the cards themselves will not keep the content inside them.

In the background there is a video. You can customise this with a YouTube, Vimeo, DailyMotion, or other web video service links. The purpose of the video is not to grab your attention but to provide a nice background to focus.
